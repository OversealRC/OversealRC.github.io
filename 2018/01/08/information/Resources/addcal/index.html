Adding to the Calendar

Instructions below cover adding runs to the calendar so they sync to the main JL calendar on the website

iPhone
iPad
PC
Android

iPhone

  1. First open the google calendar app

  1. Next, select the ‘+’ icon in the bottom right corner

  1. Select ‘Event’

  1. Enter your name and intended distance covered as the title

  1. If your ORC calendar isn’t selected, tap ‘Event’ and select it

  1. Make sure ‘All Day’ is checked and that both the start and end dates are the day you will be (co) leading.

  1. Press ‘Save’

iPad

PC

  1. Using a web browser, navigate to calendar.google.com and log in.

  1. Click the ‘+ Create’ button in the top left corner

  1. Enter your name and intended distance covered as the title

  1. If your ORC calendar isn’t selected, click next to the calendar icon and select it

  1. Click ‘More’

  1. Make sure ‘All Day’ is checked and that both the start and end dates are the day you will be (co) leading.

  1. Click ‘Save’

About

What started as a post on Facebook asking if anyone would be interested in meeting to run or jog on a Sunday morning, is now a friendly, community spirited running group based in the heart of The National Forest.
