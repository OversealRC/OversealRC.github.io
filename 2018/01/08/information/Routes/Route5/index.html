Route Five

Co-leader approved route

Download route card

Distance

5 KM

Terrain

Pavement & Trail.

Obstacles

Pedestrian/cycles gates.

Hazards

Road crossing at – Burton road (Robin Hood & Admiral fish bar) Hallcroft Ave, Slackey Lane, Bath Yard and Moira road.

Directions:

• On leaving the Overseal Village Hall car park turn Left onto Woodville road. Staying on the pavement, approximately 120 meters cross over Hallcroft Ave and continue along Woodvile road until you reach the crossroads at the Church and the Robin Hood pub. (This is TAP 1)

• At the crossroads use the crossing point to cross over Burton road and onto Lullington Rd, keeping to the Left hand footpath. Follow Lullington road for approximately 200 meters, where you will turn Left onto Valley Rd. Keeping to the right hand pavement continue on Valley Rd for approximately 450 meters, where you will come to the crossroads with Burton Rd. the Admiral Fish bar will be just to the left hand side on Burton Rd. (This is TAP 2)

• Carefully cross straight over the road onto Moira Rd. staying on the right side pavement continue for approximately 350 meters where you will get to the junction to Hallcroft Ave. (This is TAP 3)

• Cross over Hallcroft Ave and still on the right hand pavement; continue along Moira Road, running past the junction to Shortheath on your Right. Continue for a further 530 meters along Moira road until you reach the junction to Slackey Lane. Cross over Slackey lane and continue for approximately 100 meters and you will reach the entrance to the YHA on the left hand side. And the start of Bath Lane (This is TAP 4)

• From the entrance to the YHA continue along Bath Lane, under the Bridge and slightly down hill, for approximately 250 meters where you will come to the junction to Bath Yard (Conkers waterfront) cross over Bath yard and continue for approximately 400 meters, where you will come to a sweeping left hand bend and the junction to Shortheath Rd. (This is TAP 5)

• Cross over the road onto Shortheath Rd, still staying on the right hand side pavement. Approximately after 270 meters you will run past the junction to Donisthorpe Ln on your Left. Continue along Shortheath Rd for approximately 360 meters, then the road will start to go up hill slightly, with a row of terrace houses on the Right. At the top of the road it will bend sharply to the Right. (This is TAP 6)

• From the sharp bend in the road, continue on Shortheath road for approximately 440 meters where you will come to the junction of Moira Rd. (This is TAP 7)

• Turn Left and cross the road onto the right hand side pavement. Continue along Moira Rd for approximately 170 meters until you reach the junction of Hallcroft Ave. Turn Right onto Hallcroft Ave and follow the road for a further 390 meters to the junction with Woodvile Rd. Turn Right onto Woodvile road and the Overseal village hall is approximately 140 meters on the Right (This is TAP 8)

About

Founded in 2015, we are a friendly running group catering for all levels.

What started as a post on Facebook asking if anyone would be interested in meeting to run or jog on a Sunday morning, is now a friendly, community spirited running group based in the heart of The National Forest.
